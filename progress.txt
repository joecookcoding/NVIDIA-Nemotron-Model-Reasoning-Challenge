## Codebase Patterns

- Final competition submissions are `submission.zip`, not `submission.csv`.
- The submitted artifact must be a compatible LoRA adapter for `NVIDIA Nemotron-3-Nano-30B`.
- Evaluation uses `vLLM` and expects `adapter_config.json` in the submitted adapter package.
- Competition rules currently captured in repo allow `5` submissions per day and up to `2` final submissions.
- Prize eligibility requires a public Kaggle notebook and a write-up.
- The current preferred first-submit path is `notebooks/nvidia-nemotron-submission-demo.ipynb`.
